Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about consultations and services

How do I schedule my first consultation?

You can schedule your consultation by calling 0303-5229634, sending a WhatsApp message, or filling out the contact form above. I'll get back to you within 24 hours to confirm your appointment.

What should I expect during the first session?

The first session is an initial consultation where we'll discuss your concerns, goals, and determine the best approach for your treatment. This typically lasts 60-90 minutes and includes a comprehensive assessment.

Do you offer online consultations?

Yes, I offer both in-person and online consultations to accommodate your needs and preferences. Online sessions are conducted via secure video platforms to ensure privacy and confidentiality.

How long does therapy typically last?

The duration of therapy varies depending on individual needs and goals. Some clients benefit from short-term therapy (6-12 sessions), while others may require longer-term support. We'll discuss this during your initial consultation.

Is my information kept confidential?

Absolutely. All information shared during our sessions is strictly confidential and protected by professional ethics and legal requirements. I will only share information with your explicit consent or in specific legal circumstances.
